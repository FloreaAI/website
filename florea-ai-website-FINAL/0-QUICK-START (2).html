üéØ 5-Minute Setup

Step 1: Open js/data.js

This is where you'll make most of your changes. Open it in any text editor (Notepad, TextEdit, VS Code, etc.)

Step 2: Add Your Team Members

Find the teamMembers section and update the sample data with your actual team members. Just change the text inside the quotes!

Example:

name: "Dr. Your Name",
role: "Your Role",
email: "[email protected]",

Step 3: Add Your Publications

Find the publications section. Copy an existing entry and modify it with your publication details.

Step 4: Customize Colors (Optional)

Open css/styles.css and find the :root section at the top. Change the color codes to match your preferences.

Step 5: Update Contact Info

Open contact.html and update the email, office location, and social media links.
